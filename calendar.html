Programs

Uplift Foundation offers youth, that have no access to their chosen wants or desires, an opportunity to participate and work with caring mentors to improve their ability to develop a positive attitude towards their future. The long-term goal of Uplift Foundation is to empower the young person to break the habits that are leading to trouble in school and the community.

Events

Uplift Foundation will provide programs that is in direct response to the growing number of youth that are either falling through the cracks at school or are already entangled with extra curricular activities due to boredom, not being challenged, or not having the resources to pursue their personal goals. The goal of the program is to build our youth to become strong fearless leaders, dream chasers & barrier breakers, while teaching discipline, respect & hard work that will transition them to adulthood and offer a positive support system to avoid the pitfalls that can derail their lives. The focus is slightly different at each level but the goal remains the same; empower the youth to make positive changes in his/her life.
